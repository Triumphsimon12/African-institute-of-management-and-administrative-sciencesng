About Us

African Institute of Management and Administrative Sciences is a premier Institution for Management and Administrative Sciences in Africa.It was incorporated and established with the Corporate Affairs Commission under the Companies and Allied Matters Act 2020 with registration No:7478267, Approved by the Federal Ministry of justice and Guaranteed by National Board of Technical Education , Nigeria,West Africa

Our certification programs and membership grades for the African Institute of Management and Administrative Sciences (AIMAS):

Certification Programs:

1. Certified Management Practitioner (CMP)

2. Certified Administrative Professional (CAP)

3. Certified Leadership Professional (CLP)

4. Certified Strategic Management Professional (CSMP)

5. Certified Innovation and Entrepreneurship Professional (CIEP)

Membership Grades:


1. Student Member (SM)

2. Affiliate Member (AM)

3. Associate Member (AIMAS)

4. Full Member (MIMAS)

5. Fellow Member (FIMAS)

6. Honorary Fellow (HFMAS) - conferred on distinguished individuals who have made significant contributions to management and administrative sciences.

Requirements for each membership grade:


- Student Member (SM): Enrolled in a management or administration program

- Affiliate Member (AM): Interested in management and administration, but without qualifications

- Associate Member (AIMAS): Holds a relevant degree or certification, with some work experience

- Full Member (MIMAS): Meets specific work experience and qualification requirements

- Fellow Member (FIMAS): Demonstrates significant achievements and contributions to the field

- Honorary Fellow (HFMAS): Conferred by the Institute's Council on distinguished individuals.

Our goals for establishing the African Institute of Management and Administrative Sciences (AIMAS):

1. Capacity Building: Enhance the capacity of African professionals in management and administration to meet international standards.

2. Knowledge Hub: Establish AIMAS as a hub for knowledge creation, sharing, and dissemination in management and administrative sciences in Africa.

3. Professional Development: Provide training, certification, and continuous professional development opportunities for management and administrative professionals in Africa.

4. Research and Innovation: Conduct cutting-edge research and promote innovation in management and administrative sciences to address African challenges.

5. Networking: Foster a network of management and administrative professionals across Africa to facilitate collaboration, mentorship, and knowledge sharing.

6. Policy Influence: Inform and influence policy decisions in Africa through evidence-based research and advocacy.

7. Excellence Awards: Establish annual awards to recognize and reward outstanding achievements in management and administration in Africa.

8. Strategic Partnerships: Collaborate with international organizations, universities, and research institutions to advance management and administrative sciences in Africa.

9. Accreditation: Achieve international accreditation for AIMAS programs and certifications to ensure global recognition.

10. Sustainability: Ensure the long-term sustainability of AIMAS through strategic planning, resource mobilization, and effective governance.

11. Digital Presence: Establish a robust digital platform for online learning, knowledge sharing, and community engagement.

12. Pan-African Reach: Expand AIMAS programs and services to all African countries, promoting regional integration and cooperation.

These goals are designed to position AIMAS as a leading institution in Africa, dedicated to enhancing management and administrative capacities, promoting excellence, and driving economic growth and development.
